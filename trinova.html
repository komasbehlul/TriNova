Our Story

TriNova was founded on a simple idea: fashion should be honest. No noise, no excess. Just quality you can feel.

Where it began

Founded in 2026, TriNova started as a reaction to an industry obsessed with volume over value. We set out to build something different: a brand built on restraint, quality, and truth. Every piece we make is designed to last, to fit well, and to feel effortless.

🧵

How we make things

We work directly with manufacturers in Portugal and Turkey who share our standards. Every fabric is tested for durability and comfort before it enters our supply chain. We produce in small batches to reduce waste and ensure consistent quality.
